# 🚀 Welcome to Skill-Sharp Academy

![Skill-Sharp Academy Logo](https://example.com/logo.png)

## Unleash the Future of Education

Welcome to Skill-Sharp Academy, where we empower learners and educators alike with our cutting-edge Student and Teacher Management System. This repository is your portal to a world of educational innovation, efficiency, and excellence.

## 📚 Explore the System

### Students 🎓

At the heart of our academy are our students, and we've designed the Student Management System with them in mind:

- **Effortless Enrollment**: Easily onboard new students with their personal details.
- **Age on Autopilot**: Watch as the system calculates ages automatically, like magic.
- **Personalization**: Students can update their profiles to stay connected and engaged.
- **Fortified Security**: Protect student data with robust password authentication.
- **City Diversity**: Discover the rich tapestry of cities our students hail from.

### Teachers 👩‍🏫👨‍🏫

Our dedicated educators light the path to knowledge. The Teacher Management System empowers them:

- **Seamless Staffing**: Effortlessly add new teachers to our esteemed roster.
- **Age at a Glance**: Witness age calculations happening in an instant.
- **Profile Power**: Teachers can easily manage their profiles, ensuring seamless communication.
- **Data Protection**: Rest assured, teacher data is safeguarded with top-notch password security.
- **City Insights**: Keep an eye on the diverse cities our educators call home.
